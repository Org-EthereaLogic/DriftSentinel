## Interpretation

Verified facts:

- The run executed against real parquet workload files, not demo generators,
  mock tables, or synthetic inputs.
- The drift policy methods were honored in runtime after the fix; the evidence
  records `wasserstein` for each monitored column.
- The app surfaces read the real registry and evidence artifacts generated by
  the same run.

Residual precision caveat:

- Evidence currently rounds drift scores to 4 decimals, so extremely similar
  real distributions display as `1.0` even when the raw normalized score is
  slightly below 1.0. This did not affect the gate verdict in this run, but it
  does reduce operator-visible precision.
